# lumen 🕯️
a cozy game about collecting *whispers* of light and restoring life to the world. deposit whispers in the well and watch the world be revived!

## controls
- a/d or arrow keys: move
- w or space: jump
- left click or enter: interact

## 💿 about the project
### built with
- godot, resprite

### features
- collect whispers to restore color and life to the world
- walking and resting animations

## 🛒 how to view
you can download or play the game at: [https://ela11ne.itch.io/lumen]
best viewed on laptop
